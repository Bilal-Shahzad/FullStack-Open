# Question Everything

- Debugging Full Stack applications requires a systematic approach.
- Identify where the problem occurs, question everything, and eliminate possibilities.
- Logging, tools like Postman, debuggers, and experience play crucial roles.
- The "stop and fix" principle helps avoid introducing more bugs.
